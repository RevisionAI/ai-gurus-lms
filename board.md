Blackboard Learn is a comprehensive learning management system (LMS) designed to facilitate online, hybrid, and web-enhanced teaching and learning. Its core features support course delivery, student engagement, assessment, and administrative integration. Below is a list of the basic features:

Course Management

Instructors can create, organize, and manage courses, including posting syllabi, assignments, tests, and multimedia content such as files, images, audio, and video.

Courses are accessible through a centralized "Courses List," where users see all courses they are enrolled in or assigned to.

Content Organization

Learning modules, folders, and lesson plans help structure and organize course materials for easy navigation.

Content can include documents, videos, SCORM packages, and links to external resources.

Assessment Tools

Instructors can create and manage assignments, quizzes, tests, and surveys (graded or ungraded).

Customizable grading and feedback options, including native plagiarism detection tools.

Students can view their grades and feedback through a dedicated "My Grades" section.

Communication and Collaboration

Built-in discussion boards, forums, and group tools for collaborative learning and peer interaction.

Announcements and messaging features for instructor-student communication.

Integration with video conferencing tools for live virtual classes.

Analytics and Reporting

Comprehensive analytics provide insights into student performance, engagement, and course effectiveness.

Skill gap analysis and competency tracking for personalized learning and professional development.

Accessibility and Personalization

Integrated accessibility tools and an intuitive, inclusive interface that works across devices.

Customizable dashboards and landing pages, such as the Activity Stream, which highlights new content, deadlines, and important updates.

Integration and Extensibility

Open architecture allows integration with student information systems, authentication protocols, and third-party educational tools.

Seamless support for standards like SCORM and compliance tracking.

Administrative and Support Features

Role-based access control for students, instructors, and administrators.

Dedicated support resources, including tutorials, documentation, and community forums.

Flexible deployment options: cloud-based, locally hosted, or as a managed service.

Mobile and Remote Learning

Mobile apps and responsive design enable access to courses and materials from any device, supporting remote and flexible learning.

Customization and Flexibility

Highly customizable interface and tools to suit various instructional models (fully online, hybrid, or web-enhanced).

Instructors can tailor course menus, content visibility, and tool availability.
